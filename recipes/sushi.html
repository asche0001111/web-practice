yummy yum sushi :3

Description:

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Nori (seaweed) sheets
  • Fresh fish slices (salmon, tuna, etc.)
  • Avocado, thinly sliced
  • Cucumber, julienned
  • Soy sauce for dipping
  • Pickled ginger and wasabi for serving

Instructions:

  1. Cook sushi rice according to package instructions.
  2. In a small bowl, mix rice vinegar, sugar, and salt. Fold into the cooked rice.
  3. Place a nori sheet on a bamboo sushi rolling mat.
  4. Spread a thin layer of seasoned rice on the nori, leaving a border at the top.
  5. Add fish slices, avocado, and cucumber along the bottom edge of the rice.
  6. Roll tightly using the bamboo mat, sealing the edge with a bit of water.
  7. Slice the roll into bite-sized pieces using a sharp knife.
  8. Repeat with remaining ingredients.
  9. Serve with soy sauce, pickled ginger, and wasabi. Enjoy your homemade sushi!
